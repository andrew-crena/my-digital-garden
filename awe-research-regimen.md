## Your Weekly Schedule (7.5 hours total)

### Monday (60 min) - Build Queue
1. Pick this week's theme
2. Find 8–10 papers
3. Write one guiding question

Example: "Does music-evoked awe increase prosocial behavior beyond positive affect, and does absorption moderate this?"

### Tuesday (90 min) - Triage 5 Papers
Skim each paper (15–18 min), fill row in spreadsheet, write one-line takeaway

What to capture: induction type, awe measure, comparison condition, behavioral vs self-report outcome, key result

### Wednesday (90 min) - Deep Read 2 Papers
Pick 2 best papers from Tuesday, complete full analysis

Ask: Is awe specific? Good control? Behavioral outcome?

### Thursday (90 min) - Extract Methods
Choose best paper this week, copy exact procedures, note what you'd steal for your own study

### Friday (60 min) - Write Synthesis
300–400 words: What we know (2–3 findings), What's uncertain (2–3 gaps), My study idea (1 paragraph)

### Saturday (60 min) - Application Work
Odd weeks: Draft SOP paragraph | Even weeks: Write PI-fit proposal

### Sunday (30 min) - Reset
Update Top 15 list, write 5-line recap, pick next theme

## Research Quality Checklist

For every paper, ask:

1. Is this actually awe? (vastness + accommodation, not just "inspiring")
2. Did they rule out positive affect, elevation, arousal?
3. Behavioral outcome or just self-report?
4. Mechanism tested with proper timing?
5. WEIRD sample? Generalizes to art/collective contexts?
6. Where could traits moderate? (absorption, openness, etc.)
7. What can I reuse? (induction, measure, design)

## Your Tracking System

### Research Map (Spreadsheet)
One row per paper with these columns:

Citation | Year | Lab | Domain | Context | Induction | Measure | Comparison | DV Type | Mechanism | Moderators | Flags | Verdict | Tag | Takeaway

Domain: nature/music/art/ritual/VR
Context: individual/co-present/synchronized collective
Measure: DPES/AWE-S/single-item
DV Type: behavioral/self-report
Mechanism: small self/connectedness/meaning/accommodation
Verdict: A (must-read) / B (useful) / C (skip)
Tag: theory/measure/method/evidence/moderator

After 8 weeks you can instantly find: "All music studies with behavioral outcomes" or "All studies testing absorption as moderator"

## Sample Study Ideas

### Study 1: Concert Awe × Absorption
Question: Does live music create more prosocial behavior than recordings, especially for high-absorption people?

Design: 2 (live vs recorded) × absorption (continuous)
Outcome: Donation to venue
Why: Field setting, behavioral DV, theory-driven moderator

### Study 2: Museum Art × Expertise
Question: Do art experts and novices experience different social effects from aesthetic awe?

Design: Field study in museum
Outcome: Prosocial intentions, belonging
Why: Ecological validity, expertise moderator

### Study 3: Ritual × Culture
Question: Does collective ritual awe work differently for collectivists vs individualists?

Design: 2 (collective vs solo) × self-construal
Outcome: Group identification
Why: Cultural moderator, collective context
